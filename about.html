A Lifetime of Devotion

A Life Dedicated to Divine Music

Vidwan B. Raghunath's journey in music is not merely a career but a lifelong penance (Sadhana). Born in 1962, he dedicated his life to Carnatic Music under the tutelage of his father, Vidwan Sri B. Venkateswarlu.

Matru Devo Bhava | Pitru Devo Bhava

Honoring the sacred Guru-Shishya Parampara

Other Distinguished Credentials

  • Participated in Srivari Brahmotsavams at Tirumala, Sri Thyagaraja Festivals & Annamacharya Festivals & Recordings of T.T.D.
  • Performed in major music concerts across India including Madras, Delhi, Kolkata, Bombay, Bangalore, and Kavaratti (Lakshadweep).

Today, as a Grade I Artist at the Annamacharya Project (T.T.D.), he continues to serve the Lord through his music while nurturing the next generation of artists at Sri Annamayya Sankeerthana Academy.
